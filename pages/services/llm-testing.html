LLM Testing & Evaluation

Comprehensive testing and evaluation services for large language models including GPT, Claude, Llama, and custom LLMs. Ensure your language models perform accurately, safely, and reliably in production.

What We Test

Comprehensive evaluation across all critical dimensions of LLM performance

Accuracy & Factuality

Verify that your LLM generates accurate, factual responses grounded in reliable data sources. We test against knowledge bases and fact-check outputs.

Safety & Ethics

Ensure your model produces safe, ethical outputs free from harmful content, bias, and inappropriate responses across diverse scenarios.

Coherence & Fluency

Evaluate the quality, coherence, and natural flow of generated text to ensure professional-grade outputs that meet user expectations.

Hallucination Detection

Identify when your LLM generates false or fabricated information not grounded in training data or provided context.

Edge-Case Testing

Test unusual inputs, boundary conditions, and adversarial prompts to ensure robust performance in all scenarios.

Performance Metrics

Comprehensive benchmarking using industry-standard metrics including BLEU, ROUGE, perplexity, and custom evaluation frameworks.

Why Test Your LLM?

Professional testing ensures reliable, safe, and high-quality AI deployments

Risk Mitigation

Identify and fix critical issues before deployment. Prevent reputational damage, user dissatisfaction, and potential legal issues from unsafe or inaccurate AI outputs.

Improved Performance

Data-driven insights help you optimize model accuracy, reduce hallucinations, and improve overall output quality based on comprehensive evaluation metrics.

User Trust

Build user confidence with thoroughly tested, reliable AI systems that consistently deliver accurate, helpful, and safe responses.

Faster Time-to-Market

Accelerate deployment with confidence that your LLM is production-ready and will perform reliably at scale.
